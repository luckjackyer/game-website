How to Play

Goal

Defeat enemies, collect gold, and become stronger! Battle your way through dungeons and defeat powerful bosses!

How to Play

  • ⚔️ Click the enemy to attack
  • 💰 Collect gold from defeated enemies
  • ⬆️ Buy upgrades to become stronger
  • 🎒 Find items from enemies (10% drop rate)
  • 📈 Level up every 10 kills for more HP
  • 🤖 Buy Auto Attack to fight automatically

Tips

  • Focus on upgrading your weapon first
  • Auto Attack helps when you're away
  • Enemies get stronger as you level up
