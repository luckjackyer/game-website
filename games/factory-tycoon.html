How to Play

Goal

Build and manage factories to generate passive income. Expand your business empire!

How to Play

  • 🏭 Buy factories to start generating income
  • 💰 Each factory produces income per second
  • ⬆️ Upgrade factories to increase production
  • 🔧 Buy global upgrades to boost all factories
  • 💾 Your game auto-saves every 30 seconds

Tips

  • Balance your investments between factories and upgrades
  • Higher-level factories cost more but produce more
  • Global upgrades affect ALL your factories!
