    Advanced Upgrades and Prestige Systems

    For players who delve deep into the game, **Burger Clicker** offers **advanced upgrades** that significantly alter the economics of your burger business. These might include **global marketing campaigns** that boost demand exponentially, **research and development labs** that unlock proprietary burger recipes with higher profit margins, or **strategic partnerships** with other virtual businesses. These high-tier upgrades require substantial investment but yield truly astronomical returns, allowing you to reach new pinnacles of wealth and influence within the game's universe.

    A common feature in many idle and incremental games, and certainly present in Burger Clicker, is a **prestige system**. This mechanic allows you to reset your game progress in exchange for **permanent bonuses** that make future playthroughs significantly faster and more lucrative. When you choose to **prestige**, you typically lose your current buildings and upgrades but gain **prestige points** or a special currency. These points can then be spent on **permanent global upgrades** that affect all future game sessions, such as a permanent increase in all earnings, a boost to upgrade efficiency, or unlocking special features earlier. This system is designed to provide a sense of continuous progression even after reaching the perceived "end" of a regular playthrough, offering incredible replayability and encouraging players to experiment with different strategies.
